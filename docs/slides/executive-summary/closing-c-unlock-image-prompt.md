# AI Image Generation Prompt: "The Unlock"

## Primary Prompt

```
A dramatic visualization of a glowing digital lock transforming into an open state, releasing streams of light and energy that flow outward in multiple directions. The lock is centered in the composition, rendered in luminous green (#10b981) with a ethereal glow.

From the opening lock, five distinct streams of colored light burst outward like rays of possibility:
- A blue stream (#3b82f6) flowing upward, representing innovation and speed
- A green stream (#10b981) flowing to the upper right, representing growth and scaling
- A purple stream (#8b5cf6) flowing to the lower right, representing AI intelligence
- An orange stream (#f97316) flowing to the lower left, representing automation and efficiency
- A cyan stream (#06b6d4) flowing to the upper left, representing connectivity and flow

The background is a deep dark navy (#0a0f1a) with subtle radial gradients of blue and purple creating depth. Floating particles of light scatter across the scene, suggesting the abundance of opportunities being released.

The overall mood is optimistic, expansive, and futuristic. The image should feel like a moment of transformation - the instant when potential becomes reality.

Style: Modern digital art, clean and professional, suitable for an executive presentation. Avoid cartoon-like elements. The aesthetic should be sleek, tech-forward, and inspiring.

Aspect ratio: 16:9 (widescreen, suitable for presentation slides)
```

## Visual Guidelines

1. **Central Focus**: The lock should be the clear focal point, but not overwhelming - the streams of possibility are equally important
2. **Motion**: The image should feel dynamic, like energy is actively flowing outward
3. **Balance**: Streams should radiate somewhat evenly to create visual balance
4. **Depth**: Use gradients and particle effects to create a sense of 3D space
5. **Professional Tone**: Avoid overly decorative or playful elements - this is for executives
6. **Inspiration**: The image should evoke feelings of opportunity, potential, and forward momentum
