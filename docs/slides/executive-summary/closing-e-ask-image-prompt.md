# AI Image Generation Prompt: "The Simple Ask"

## Concept
A sophisticated visual representing three decisive moments converging into a single clear path forward. The image should convey the elegance of Socratic questioning - where the right questions lead inevitably to the right answer.

---

## Primary Prompt

```
A dark, sophisticated abstract visualization representing three key decisions converging into unity. Deep navy-black background (#0a0f1a). Three elegant glowing question marks or decision nodes arranged in a gentle arc, each a different color: electric blue (#3b82f6), emerald green (#10b981), and rich purple (#8b5cf6).

Each decision node emits soft light rays that converge toward a central focal point below them - a luminous gateway or doorway shape representing "the answer" or "the path forward." The convergence point glows with a gradient of all three colors blending together.

Subtle geometric patterns - clean lines, minimal circles, and elegant connecting threads - link the three nodes, suggesting logical flow and interconnection. Small checkmark symbols or affirmation indicators float near each node, rendered in soft white (#f1f5f9).

The overall composition suggests certainty, clarity, and the inevitable conclusion of good reasoning. Executive boardroom aesthetic - minimal, powerful, decisive. High contrast against the dark background. No text. Cinematic lighting with soft glows and subtle lens flare effects.

Style: Modern corporate visualization, abstract geometric, premium tech aesthetic, 4K quality, ultra-clean lines, sophisticated minimalism.
```

## Alternative Prompts

### Version A: Three Paths, One Destination
```
Abstract visualization of three illuminated pathways converging on a dark navy background (#0a0f1a). Each path begins as a distinct colored light trail - blue (#3b82f6), green (#10b981), purple (#8b5cf6) - flowing from the top of the image. The paths curve gracefully and merge at a bright central point that pulses with combined radiance.

Along each path, a single elegant checkpoint or node glows softly, representing a question answered. Subtle particle effects suggest motion and momentum. The convergence point at the bottom forms a stylized arrow or doorway pointing forward, symbolizing "the next step."

Minimalist, premium, executive aesthetic. Clean geometric forms. Soft ambient glow effects. No text or words. Photorealistic quality with abstract elements.
```

### Version B: The Decision Matrix
```
Three floating holographic cards or panels arranged in a staggered formation against a deep dark background (#0a0f1a). Each card has a distinct accent color glow - first card blue (#3b82f6), second green (#10b981), third purple (#8b5cf6).

Each card contains an abstract checkmark or approval symbol, softly glowing. Thin luminous lines connect the cards in sequence, suggesting logical progression. Below the three cards, a larger unified symbol emerges from their combined light - perhaps an abstract key, gateway, or "start" button rendered in blended gradients of all three colors.

The composition suggests a decision tree that has reached its conclusion. Premium glass-like materials with subtle reflections. Ambient particles floating in the dark space. Executive presentation aesthetic. No text.
```

### Version C: The Affirmation Sequence
```
Abstract representation of three "yes" moments in sequence, visualized as three concentric ripples or pulses emanating from bright nodes. Dark navy void background (#0a0f1a).

First ripple in electric blue (#3b82f6), second in emerald (#10b981), third in violet (#8b5cf6). The ripples overlap and intersect, creating interference patterns that form a bright central mandala or focal point where all three colors blend into white-gold light.

Geometric precision with organic flow. Suggests inevitability and harmony - when three truths align, a single answer emerges. Subtle grid lines in the background suggest structure and logic. Premium, sophisticated, decisive mood.

Style: High-end tech visualization, sacred geometry influence, executive presentation quality. No text.
```
